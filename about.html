I'm 29 years old and live in Omaha, Nebraska (middle of the US). I am currently a college student working on my bachelors in IT Innovation at UNO (University of Nebraska at Omaha.)For those who do not know what IT Innovation is, a simple explanation is that it is a hands on program that is preparing students for the future. In the IT Innovation major, students learn basic IT skills, such as programming, cyber security, application development, statistics, business practices, etc. In addition, the major allows us freedom to chose any elective class offered by the University, as long as it will help our degree program and give it direction, and as long as it is relevant to the IT Innovation degree.

The 11 elective classes, which is called the area of emphasis, is where many of my fun and exploratory classes come in. Through my area of emphasis, I am taking a few journalism classes, an entrepreneurship class, digital art classes, a creative writing class, and one or two international studies courses. Going forward into the future, I have many classes coming up that will improve my current skills and teach me new ones.

I am set to graduate in May 2023, which is only 1 and a half years away! With only 10 classes left, it feels so close!

After I graduate I plan to go to earn my Masters degree.
